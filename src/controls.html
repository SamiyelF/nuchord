NUCHORD - Always In Tune

Controls
Chord Playing:
awsedrf - chord variant (tonic, dominant, etc)
arrow keys - chord modifier (major, diminished, etc)

Sound Control:
1234 - waveform selection (sine, saw, square, triangle)
t/g - volume up/down

Effects:
b - open effects settings menu
Or use Settings > Effects Settings from the menu bar

Other:
ESC - quit application
